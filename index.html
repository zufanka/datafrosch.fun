Ada Homolova

Data Journalist & Educator

Hi! I'm Ada. I am a freelance data journalist, data journalism teacher, speaker and public spending enthusiast. With over 10 years of experience in data journalism, I have a lot of stories based on small and big datasets and I am here to share what I learned along the way.

Since the start of my career, I have been teaching data skills, as I believe more journalists should be able to use them. I founded Data Frosch by accident, because I bought a frog hat last winter. It allows me a fun way to put my knowledge out there and help others find me.

If you are curious about my extended portfolio have a look at my website. You can keep in touch via LinkedIN, or my personal newsletter below!

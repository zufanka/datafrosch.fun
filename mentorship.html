What you get

Book a single session to unblock a specific problem, or several to go deeper. Either way, we work on what you bring and build exactly the skills you need.

Booked around you

Ada Homolova

Professional frosch

Hi! I'm Ada, a freelance data & investigative journalist and teacher. Over more than 10 years I've made a lot of stories with small and big datasets, and I'm here to share what I learned along the way.

I've always loved helping other journalists get comfortable with data, because I believe these skills should be for everyone. I've taught at conferences like Dataharvest and NICAR, given guest lectures in the Master's program in Gothenburg, and mentored journalists 1:1 through programs like IJ4EU.

I founded Data Frosch by accident, because I bought a frog hat last winter. 🐸
